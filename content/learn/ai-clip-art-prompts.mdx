Great AI clip art starts with a great prompt. The more specific you are about subject, style, color, and mood, the better your result. Below are 50 prompts you can copy and paste directly into clip.art — organized by category so you can jump to what you need.

## How prompts work in clip.art

When you type a prompt, our AI interprets your description and generates a unique image. You also pick a **style** (Flat, Outline, Cartoon, Sticker, Vintage, or Watercolor) which shapes the visual output. Think of the prompt as the "what" and the style as the "how."

**Tip:** Be specific about the subject and action. "Cat" is fine, but "orange tabby cat wearing a chef hat, holding a whisk" gives you something far more useful.

## Tips for better prompts

1. **Be specific** — "red sports car" beats "car"
2. **Describe the scene** — add background, lighting, or context
3. **Mention colors** — helps the AI nail the palette
4. **Keep it to one subject** — multiple subjects can confuse the output
5. **Experiment with styles** — the same prompt in Flat vs. Watercolor looks completely different
