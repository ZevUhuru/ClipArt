Creating custom clip art with AI takes three steps: describe, generate, download. This quick walkthrough shows you exactly how it works.

## Three steps to custom clip art

1. **Describe** — Type what you want in plain English. The more specific you are, the better the result. "Happy sun wearing sunglasses" works great.
2. **Pick a style** — Choose from Flat, Outline, Cartoon, Sticker, Vintage, Watercolor, or 3D. Each style gives the same prompt a completely different look.
3. **Download** — Your image is ready in seconds. Download it as a PNG and use it anywhere — no attribution required.
